About Doodle World

Doodle World, a Roblox game created by Doodle World Studios released on the 2nd of April,
2022. Explore a mysterious island that is inhabited by strange and magical creatures named
Doodles! Begin your own adventure with your very own Starter Doodle and compete in a
competition against other trainers to collect all 10 Keys!
